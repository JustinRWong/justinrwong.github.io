About Me

I Strive to Make the Most Out of Every Opportunity and Every Day.

Currently As A....

Computer Science Student at UC Berkeley, I'm exposed to many different fields that excite me. From emerging technologies, such as machine learning and blockchain, becoming productized in startups to learning from the theoretical and practical classes under the CS curriculum, I'm immersed in the environment that not only inspires me to be a technologist, but also create lasting, meaningful friendships and connections with peers, University faculty, and Berkeley network alike.



Lead Student Coordinator, I lead the organization of the one week Berkeley Method of Entrepreneurship Bootcamp(INDENG 192) , which teaches Berkeley students and global participants how to transform an idea into a compelling story that reveals a problem which can be solved with a product-market fit solution and executable business model. The bi-annual bootcamp has about 100 Berkeley students about 30-40 global participants from 10+ different countries, including Brazil, Spain, Finland, Netherlands, Taiwan, China, and Hong Kong. I took the class myself in Spring 2018 and enjoyed being among amazing people and had the opportunity to go to Hong Kong to be a student mentor at Hong Kong Baptist University. In 2020, I'll be leading 10-15 Berkeley students to be student mentors at HKBU for another entreneurship bootcamp.

My goals here are to expand the Berkeley chapter past the walls of UC Berkeley and into other universities, high schools, and local communities.
